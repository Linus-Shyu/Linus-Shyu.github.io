Overview ‹ Young Agent Lab

Advancing human capability by designing agent systems that learn, plan, and collaborate in the real world

Overview

The Young Agent Lab creates and evaluates new ways of bringing together tool-using agents, applied machine learning, and human-centered product design to help people build, learn, and flourish.

Our primary motivation is to make intelligent systems that stay practical — fast enough to deploy, reliable enough to trust, and structured enough to improve over time. We study how young builders and researchers can use agents to turn ideas into software, how emotion-aware applications can support wellbeing, and how competitive engineering keeps implementation grounded in real constraints.

Some of our work contributes to basic science, including manuscripts under peer review and open-source tools; other projects advance research outside the lab, with applications aimed at improving everyday learning, creativity, and technical independence.
