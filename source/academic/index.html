About Me

I am Linus Shyu (许发鑫), a high school student from Qinghai, China, passionate about computer science, machine learning, and product building. I serve as Co-Founder & CTO at FeelFlow.

I build and ship real-world software while exploring applied machine learning, agent systems, and competitive programming. I share what I learn on Bilibili and YouTube, and document projects and contest writeups on my blog.

My work spans research manuscripts, hackathons, and open-source tools — I care about ideas that are both technically solid and useful in practice.
